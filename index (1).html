A cross-sectional survey comparing tinnitus-like symptoms between musicians and non-musicians ages 14–17 in Bergen County, NJ. Measured listening habits and sound exposure, then analyzed the results statistically for patterns by musician status and listening volume.

Data science · statistics

Power-Law Analysis of Spotify Artist Followers

An ongoing study using a dataset of Spotify artist metadata to test whether artist follower counts follow a power-law distribution — the same heavy-tailed pattern seen in city populations or word frequencies. Fitting and evaluating the distribution on a log–log scale, then writing up the results as a formal paper.
